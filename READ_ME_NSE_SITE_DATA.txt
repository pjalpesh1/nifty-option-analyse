NSE SITE DATA - SIMPLE USE

1. Double-click START_NSE_SITE_DATA.bat
2. Keep the black window open.
3. Open the website:
   https://pjalpesh1.github.io/nifty-option-analyse/
4. Website feed URL must be:
   data/nifty-options.dat
5. Press Load URL, then Auto Start.

This uses NSE website option-chain data directly.
No Angel One token, no Dhan token, no Excel typing.

Important:
- NSE can block/delay free website data.
- The updater will not upload blank data.
- If it repeats an NSE error, open https://www.nseindia.com/option-chain once in your browser, then restart START_NSE_SITE_DATA.bat.
